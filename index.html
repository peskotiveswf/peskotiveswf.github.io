Biography

I am an incoming Ph.D. student at MIT EECS, recently graduated from National Taiwan University (NTU) with a Bachelor’s degree in Electrical Engineering.

During my undergrad studies, I researched on question answering for listening comprehension and acoustic word embeddings under Prof. Hung-Yi Lee and Prof. Lin-Shan Lee at NTU. I also worked on machine learning problems for computer vision such as multi-label learning and transfer learning under Prof. Yu-Chiang Frank Wang at Academia Sinica/NTU.

I will be joining the Spoken Language Systems Group at MIT CSAIL this fall, working with Dr. James Glass.
